## 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm a **Senior Data Scientist** at **Indifi** with **6+ years** of hands-on experience building production ML systems in the **Fintech** space. I live at the intersection of machine learning, credit risk intelligence, and scalable automation.

Previously at **FinBox**, I led high-impact projects that shipped real results:
- 🏗️ Engineered **1,500+ features** from raw device & transactional data → **+4% AUC boost**
- ⚡ Slashed product latency by **50%** via logic optimization & pipeline restructuring
- 📉 Automated workflows with Risk-Airflow → **30% faster** product runtime
- 🌍 Built regex systems for SMS parsing across English & Vietnamese

> *"The best model is the one that runs itself."*
